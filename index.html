    DateDiff stores important dates and calculates durations between pairs of dates (either or both of which can be locked to 'today'.

    Durations are shown in years, months and days, and total number of days. They can show a duration in the past by locking Date B to 'today'; they can show a duration in the future by locking Date A to 'today'.
